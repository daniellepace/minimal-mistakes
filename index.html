I am a PhD Candidate at MIT, advised by Polina Golland.

Before pursuing my doctorate, I was an R&D Engineer at Kitware, Inc. for three years. I received my M.E.Sc. in Biomedical Engineering from Western University in London, Ontario, Canada, and my B.Cmp.H. in Biomedical Computing from Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario, Canada.
